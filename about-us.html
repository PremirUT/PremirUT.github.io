About Us

Premier University of Technology has been established for the promotion of literature, Fine Arts, Science & Technology etc. and also for the diffusion of useful knowledge. It has been started on the basis of the society registration ACT XX1 of 1860 under a department of Ministry of Commerce and Affiliated with the different institution of India, Ex. Soviet Russia & Canada. Premier University of Technology was registered & accepted by the Government of Bangladesh under society registration ACT 1860 from a department of Ministry of Commerce firstly in the name of Premier Institute of Technology bearing registration no. S-678/13 of 1979-1980. As per resolution on 09.08.1990, it has changed into Premier University of Technology. This Premier University of Technology is running under society registration ACT from 1990 and it is not running under university ACT 1992 & amendment ACT 1998/2010 and also it is not running under B.M.D.C ACT 1982. A cash has been started against U.G.C Ministry of Education, Ministry of Health, B.M.D.C & others in the Dhaka judge court vide cash no. 317/ 2003 ( Ex.278/1998). We have decided to start the Diploma level courses, under graduate level courses, Post graduate on the basis of Literature, Fine Arts, and Science & Technology etc. Programs at (1) Faidabad, Uttara, Dhaka (2) Kamlapur, Kasba, B.Baria on about 50 bigha land. Presently classes will be started on temporary basis at (1) Uttara model town,Uttara, Dhaka (2) Mothijil/ Shantinagar, Dhaka (3) CDA Avenue Muradpur/ Baperypara, Chittaging (4) Upashar, Shylet (5) Sonadanga, Khulna (6) Foolbari, Bogra & others on rented buildings. The arrangement of fund will be paid by donor Mr. Stan Lay Lavi Gran, a citizen of Vancouver, Canada for construction of the buildings. 10% poor students may get credit facility as per as agreement deed with us or with the arrangement of Banks/ Financial institution/ NGO. Only 10 % poor and brilliant regular students may get full-free, half-free, quarter free facilities from our poor fund. Payment is acceptable regarding admission fee & semester charges payable vide bank draft or payment order in favor of the Premier University of Technology. You can collect admission form by paying Tk 500/- only. Daily class arrangement accepts Friday & Saturday. Special arrangement on Friday & Saturday for PGT/ PGD/ MA/ MCom/ MS/ MSc/ MD/ MPhil/ Ph.D courses. In few courses distance education program may be arranged with 70% discount on total charged.
